Our Benefits

Dental Treatment

It is generally recommended that everyone, including young children, visit the dentist at least twice every year

Child Dentistry

Child dentistry is different from general dentistry and parents choose it as they are concerned about the oral health of their children.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Cosmetic dentistry is a method of professional oral care that focuses on improving the appearance of your teeth.

Cleaning & Polishing

No one likes to deal with dental issues. Whether it is a minor toothache or a severe gum disease, it wants fair attention.

Crown and Bridge

If you have one or more missing teeth, your dental professional may recommend that you get artificial replacements.

Full & Partial Denture

A denture is a removable replacement for missing teeth and surrounding tissues.

Dental Implant

A dental implant is basically a titanium post which is surgically inserted into the jawbone beneath the gum line to work as a tooth root

Orthodontic Treatment

Orthodontic treatment is a way of straightening or moving teeth, to improve the appearance of the teeth and how they work.

Root Canal Treatment

Root canal treatment is designed to eliminate bacteria from the infected root canal, prevent reinfection of the tooth and save the natural tooth.

Teeth Whitening

Teeth whitening is ideal for people who have healthy, unrestored teeth (no fillings) and gums.

Tooth Extraction

Excessive tooth decay, tooth infection, and crowding can all require a tooth extraction.

Tooth Filling

Dental Filling is a treatment modality to restore missing tooth structure which could have been a result of decay or trauma.

X-Ray & RVG

Dentists need radiographs. With 90% of the treatments being blind procedures, radiographs coupled with great tactile perception aid the dentist in performing high-quality dental treatment to patients.
